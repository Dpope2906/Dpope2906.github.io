Madison Wisconsin News

Have you ever wished to chance upon a piece of history? Twice? Someone did just that. One day, she noticed something sticking out of the sand while teaching a diving class.
She became unsure and felt there was more to it. She then called for assistance, and sure enough, there was a 3,000-year-old dugout boat beneath the sand.
As a maritime archeologist, this was her second discovery of an antique dugout boat!
